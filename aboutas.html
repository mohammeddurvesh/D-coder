Lernarnerz Corner

The learnerz corner is an online platform for teachers and students to interact for different subjects of engineering and lanuage learning.The platform offers different types of services such as,online teaching of different streams of engineering subjects,different regional language coaching,revision of subjects related to the university examination,helping to solve assignment problem,and computerprogramming languages training.The platform provides a way to earn in their free time for those who aspire to teach and reach student community form different parts of the world.

Vision

To make students knowledgeable and outstanding in their studies by providing excellent online training based on their need,anywhere anytime.
